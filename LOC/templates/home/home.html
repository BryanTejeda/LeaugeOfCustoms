What is League of Customs?

League of Customs is a website that helps streamline the process of matchmaking in custom games for League of Legends. If you are a League of Legends player, you know how difficult it can be to come up with a balance set of teams among your friends. League of Customs allows you to create a custom game and invite your friends to join. You can choose a variety of options to customize your game, Matchmaking based on your public game history, or even your personality types.

How does it work?

League of Customs uses the Riot Games API to gather information about your game history. It then uses this information to create a matchmaking algorithm that will create balanced teams based on your game history. You can also choose to create and account and save your personality test scores. League of Customs will then use this information to create balanced teams based on your personality types.

How do I get started?

To get started, simply click the "Create a Lobby" button. You will be prompted to enter your summoner name. Once you have entered your summoner name, you will be able to create a Lobby. You can then invite your friends to join your game, through lobby link. Your friends will be prompted to enter their summoner name, and then they will be able to join your lobby. Or you can choose to create a lobby with and matchmake using just their summoner names.
